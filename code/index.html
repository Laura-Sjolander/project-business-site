FAQ

The fee is depending on the age, type of dog and how long it has waiting for adoption.

The cost is a small representation of all that we pay for in form of veterinary bills, food, toys, equipment for the dogs, as well as the cost for rent and wages.

We have so many different dogs, longing for loving homes. There is senior dogs, dogs with disabilities, newborns and puppies, familyfriendly dogs and dogs who are scared or reactive to other dogs.
